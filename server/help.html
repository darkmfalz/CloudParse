How do you format grammars for iGG?

      It's a simple modification on Extended Backus-Naur Form! You simply do as follows:

      <Syntactic Group> ::= 'Literal' <Other Syntactic Group> | eps ;

      Keep in mind: the semicolon is used for end-of-line, not the newline.
